Member FAQs

Membership

As a SHE Wellness Club member, you gain exclusive access to private training sessions, allied health services, fitness classes, meditation sessions, personalized coaching, and a supportive community. Members also enjoy perks such as on-site culinary experts and co-working spaces.

Yes, members can bring a guest for a limited time. You can bring along your sister, mother, or best friend for a nutritious meal, a mindfulness group class, or a Pilates group class. Drop-in rates apply.

Children are welcome at SHE Wellness Club, as long as they can occupy themselves. We aim to ensure a pleasant and focused environment for all our members. Feel free to ask about any designated areas or activities suitable for your child during your visit.

Membership renewal details can be found in your member portal. If you need assistance, our front desk staff will be happy to help during your visit.

Wellness Services

Our wellness services are available from 9am to 5pm. Please book in advance to secure your preferred time slots.

Members can conveniently book up to 5 wellness services in advance through our online booking system, which is also accessible via our mobile app.

Contact and Support

For general inquiries, please visit our Contact page. You can also call us at +60 9876 5432. Our staff will be happy to assist you during our operating hours (9am - 5pm).

If you have a specific question that isn't addressed here, feel free to contact us via email or visit our front desk during operating hours. We are here to help!
