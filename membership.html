Enjoy exclusive access to curated wellness programmes for women

At our club, women benefit from a variety of personalized wellness offerings designed to enhance both physical and mental well-being. Our programmes include:

  • Private Pilates and group classes: Join our expert instructors for personalized Pilates sessions tailored to your fitness level. Engage in invigorating group classes that promote strength, flexibility, and overall wellness.
  • Nutritional guidance: Receive personalized nutritional advice and meal plans from our certified dietitians. Learn how to make informed dietary choices that support your health goals and lifestyle.
  • Themed workshops: Participate in interactive workshops focusing on various aspects of wellness, from stress management techniques to skincare routines. Explore topics that empower you to achieve holistic well-being.
  • Private chef and barista services: Indulge in gourmet meals prepared by our private chef, tailored to your dietary preferences. Enjoy artisanal coffee and specialty beverages crafted by our skilled baristas.
  • Mindfulness and meditation classes: Cultivate inner peace and mental clarity with our mindfulness and meditation classes. Learn techniques to reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance overall mindfulness in your daily life.
